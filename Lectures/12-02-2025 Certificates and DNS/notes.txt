Certificates

1. Public Key

    Everyone sees this, automatically downloaded by your browser
    .cer (only has public key, not a private)

2. Private Key

    in .pfx files (a certificate file that has both the public and private key)

    RSA
        2^2048

    Elipical Curve
        2^128

3. Subject

    CN=www.google.com

    Alternative Names
        CN=mail.google.com
        CN=*.google.com

4. Signature
    Signed by a certificate authority

makecert.exe -ks 2048 -a RSA -sub="www.google.com"
